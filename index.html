About Nicholas Solem

Nicholas Solem (Ph.D candidate @ UCSD) is the developer behind nvssynthesis. His work as nvssynthesis explores multidimensional timbral analysis & primitive synthesis techniques. He holds a Masters in Music Technology (NYU) and a BA in Philosophy (College of Wooster).
